Meet the Maker

Hi! I'm Anlet, and I'm 11 years old.

I've always loved playing with colors and creating things with my hands. One day, I started making little bracelets just for fun and gave them to my friends. They loved them so much that I decided to make more!

Now, with just a box of beads and a lot of imagination, I create bracelets that are full of love, patience, and joy.

For me, Beaded Bliss is more than just a shop — it's my way of sharing a bit of happiness with the world.

When you wear one of my bracelets, you're carrying a piece of my heart with you.
